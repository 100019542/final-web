Pokémon Super Mystery Dungeon updated on July 13

Pokémon Super Mystery Dungeon is a video game in the Pokémon Mystery Dungeon series developed by Spike Chunsoft and published by The Pokémon Company for the Nintendo 3DS handheld game console. Players control a human who has awoken as a Pokémon in a world filled entirely by the series' eponymous creatures, and must travel through random-generated dungeons completing missions and battling enemies to unravel the story.

 

Gameplay

Like its predecessor, Super Mystery Dungeon is a dungeon-crawling rogue-like role-playing game featuring 3D characters and environments. Players assume the role of one of 20 Pokémon (which include all 18 starting Pokémon from all six main series generations, along with Pikachu and Riolu), who are joined by partners who accompany them in their journey through procedurally-generated dungeons filled with enemies and traps as they help stop a major crisis and save the Pokémon world. The game features all 720 currently released Pokémon as of 2015.

 

Reception

The Japanese video game magazine Famitsu gave the game a score of 36/40 in their cross review, with the four individual reviewers all giving it a score of 9. It was the highest selling video game in Japan during its debut week, with 151,823 copies sold.
